What it does

Every signal, in one place.

Three screens that cover the full loop — from logging to understanding.

Screen 1
Log

Log blood glucose readings with type — fasting, post-meal, pre-meal, bedtime. Log meals and optionally link them to a reading. Track whether you took meds with each meal.

Screen 2
Timeline

Navigate any day and see your full signal in order — readings, meals, and meds. Quick stats for fasting and post-meal at a glance.

Screen 3
Insights

7-day trend chart, estimated HbA1c, spike pattern detection — which meals preceded readings above 180 — and a logging streak to keep you consistent.

How it works

Up in seconds.

No accounts. No syncing. Your data on your server.

01
Sign in

One shared password protects the app. No sign-up flow, no email, no OAuth. Just a secret you set in your .env.

02
Log your signal

Enter a glucose reading, tag it as fasting or post-meal, and optionally link it to what you ate. Takes under 10 seconds.

03
Track patterns

Insights surfaces spike patterns, estimates your HbA1c, and shows a 7-day trend — the more you log, the sharper the picture.
